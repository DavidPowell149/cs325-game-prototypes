Lemon Defense

  • High Concept: Defend the world's last lemon against a horde of kittens!
  • Theme: I didn't use any of the themes
  • Player Experience Goals: The rush of defending yourself against an army. Just like Gandalf would've done.
  • Gameplay: Use your mouse to aim, and click to throw water in that direction. If you hit a kitten, it will die. Kill the kittens before they touch the lemon and hurt him!
  • Prototype Goal: A defense mechanic, where a player defends himself or another object against an enemy.
  • Features: My game has personality and a lot of heart.
  • Setting/Premise: The world's last lemon has decided he can best protect himself in the middle of a field, so that's where he has stationed himself.
  • Story: Kittens like to eat lemons. Everyone knows this.
  • Target Audience: Children.
  • Play Time: It takes about 45 seconds to play if you're bad. Good players can make it last a minute or so.
