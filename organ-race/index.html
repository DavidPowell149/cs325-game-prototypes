Organ Race

  • High Concept: Avoid the walls and collect the blood cells.
  • Theme: Organ doner
  • Mandated Variety: Input: Mouse Randomness: mostly random Play style: Collector Genres: Racing; Overhead; Side-scroller
  • Player Experience Goals: I want the player to have a rush as they collect blood cells and avoid walls.
  • Gameplay: Press the up and down arrow keys to move your heart. Collect the blood cells while avoiding the walls.
  • Prototype Goal: The movement mechanic. I designed my own acceleration and implemented it.
  • Features: I think the theme of the game is unique and fun. You're sort of playing at a microscopic scale.
  • Setting/Premise: The game takes place in a blood vessel, with the heart trying to be very proactive and get its own blood cells.
  • Story: The heart realized it wasn't getting enough blood. So it willed its host to take a heart shrinking pill. Now the heart traverses its own blood vessels, and actively seeks out blood cells to keep itself healthy.
  • Target Audience: This game is for all audiences.
  • Play Time: Average play time could be a little less than a minute. It can be difficult to bypass some of the wall orientations.
  • Strategies: Planning ahead and building up some acceleration will seperate the A and B players.
